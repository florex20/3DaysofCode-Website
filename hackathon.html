What is it and how it work
    A Hackathon is an event in which people across professions gather together to solve a problem in a given period of time by making use of the technologies. During the Hackathon, programmers, designers and developers will have to work towards a solution to the problem and build a product within the given period of time.
Reminder
  • Each team must be comprised of 2 - 4 people.
  • A special award will be given out to the best team that fulfils a special requirement.
  • The details about the special requirement will be release a month before event.
  • Open to any university / college student who is currently enrolled in an undergraduate program.

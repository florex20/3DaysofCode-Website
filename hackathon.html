FAQs
  • I don't have a team, can I still join the Hackathon?
    Of course! There will be a team-forming session on the first day of the Hackathon. You can mingle with new faces and find similar minds to work together on the hack!
  • Is there a designated sleeping area?
    There will be a designated sleeping area for the participants. However, participants are to bring their own sleeping bags and toiletries if needed.
  • Is there any restriction on what programming language to be used?
    There is no restriction on the programming language to be used. The teams can develop the product in any language they desire.
